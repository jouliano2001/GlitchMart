How it all started

The team behind GlitchMart was a group of passionate web developers who expertly harnessed HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a seamless and visually engaging e-commerce platform. Using HTML, they structured the site's content for easy navigation, while CSS brought their vision to life with sleek, responsive designs that adapted to any device. JavaScript played a crucial role in adding dynamic functionality, from interactive product filters to real-time cart updates, ensuring a smooth user experience. Their collaborative effort combined creativity and technical expertise to make GlitchMart a standout in the online marketplace.
